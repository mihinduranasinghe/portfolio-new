Nobel Prize Round Table 2021

Student meet Nobel Laureate (11/02/2021)

Mihindu got this quite rare opportunity which only 16 selected students from all over the world who have shown the potential of being a scientist, and from different disciplines within science, got invitations from Nobel International Foundation to join the conversation with Professor Queloz, focusing on the topic of "being a scientist" , Organized by The Nobel Prize Org.

This was an invite-only event for hand-picked students to digitally meet a Nobel Laureate and engage in a dialogue about their life as a scientist. The session was held on a closed video conference platform

https://www.nobelprize.org/

Featuring Video

https://www.youtube.com/user/thenobelprize

About Prof. Didier Queloz

  • Didier Queloz is Professor of Physics at the University of Cambridge and Geneva University.
  • He was awarded the 2019 Nobel Prize in Physics for the 1995 discovery of exoplanet 51 Pegasi b. It was the first discovery of a planet outside our solar system orbiting a solar-type star.
  • He carried out his Nobel-Prize awarded work as a PhD student, and was awarded the Nobel Prize alongside his PhD supervisor, Michel Mayor.
