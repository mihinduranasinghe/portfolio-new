About Mihindu

Hello there,

Mihindu Ranasinghe is currently residing in Stockholm, Sweden and originally from Sri Lanka, he is a Full Stack Software Engineer at TVM Stockholm Sweden where he leverages his extensive coding experience in various technologies and also he is an Innovator and a Tech Community Volunteer with hands-on experience and specialized in Fullstack Engineering, Data Science & Machine Learning. Mihindu hold a Master of Science in Computer Science from Stockholm University and a Bachelor of Science (Hons) in Software Engineering from the University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka. His professional journey as a software engineer spans more than 5 years, during which he has had the privilege of working with reputed companies, including one of the Fortune 100 companies.

Mihindu started my career at WSO2 as a Trainee Software Engineer in 2019 and had the opportunity to work with multiple product domains such as WSO2 API Manager , WSO2 API Cloud and Choreo Cloud.

After completing his internship at WSO2, Mihindu joined Sysco LABS which is the innovation arm of Sysco, the world's largest food service company, based in the USA as a Full Stack Engineer and promoted to a >Senior Engineer Sysco Canada (USFS).

He is an active volunteer in professional engineering bodies in Sri Lanka. Mihindu serves as the present IEEE Indsutry Relations Chairperson of IEEE Sri Lanka Section and he was the Sri Lanka Section Lead at IEEEXtreme in 2020. IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing engineering and technology. Mihindu Ranasinghe is the Chair-Person of IEEE Student Branch of University of Kelaniya, Sri Lanka. Also he is a core-committee member at Facebook Developer Circles:Colombo and he contributes in various technical communities in Sri Lanka such as Google Developer Groups - GDG Sri Lanka and Google Business Groups - GBG Sri Lanka
